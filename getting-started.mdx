## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following ready:

<Callout kind="info">

- A business email address for account verification
- A CSV dataset with at least 100 rows (e.g., sales data, customer metrics)
- Modern web browser like Chrome or Firefox

</Callout>

Prepare your data in CSV format for optimal upload performance. Alchemetryx processes structured data like sales records or customer logs efficiently.

## Create Your Alchemetryx Account

Sign up takes under 2 minutes. Follow these steps to get started.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Signup Page" icon="user-plus">
    Navigate to `https://app.alchemetryx.com/signup`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Registration Form" icon="edit-3">
    Enter your business email, company name, and create a strong password. Agree to terms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification link from Alchemetryx and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Use your SME business domain email (e.g., you@yourcompany.co.uk) to unlock premium features for UK/India businesses.
</Callout>

## Log In and Explore the Dashboard

Access your dashboard at `https://dashboard.alchemetryx.com`.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Login" icon="globe">
    Enter your credentials on the login page. You'll land on the main dashboard showing recent analyses and upload options.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Bookmark Dashboard" icon="bookmark">
    Save `https://dashboard.alchemetryx.com` for quick access. Enable two-factor authentication in settings for security.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

The dashboard includes sections for datasets, insights, and reports. Key areas:

- **Datasets**: Manage uploaded files
- **Insights**: Auto-generated charts and trends
- **Reports**: Schedule exports

## Upload Your First Dataset

Upload data to start analysis. Alchemetryx supports CSV up to 50MB.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare Sample Dataset" icon="file-text">
    Create or export your data as CSV. Here's a realistic sales example:

    <CodeGroup tabs="CSV Preview,Generate with Python">
      ```csv
      date,product,units_sold,revenue,region
      2024-01-15,Laptop,5,2500,UK
      2024-01-16,Mouse,20,200,India
      2024-01-17,Keyboard,12,360,UK
      2024-01-18,Monitor,8,3200,India
      ```
      ```python
      import pandas as pd
      data = {
          'date': ['2024-01-15', '2024-01-16'],
          'product': ['Laptop', 'Mouse'],
          'units_sold': [5, 20],
          'revenue': [2500, 200],
          'region': ['UK', 'India']
      }
      df = pd.DataFrame(data)
      df.to_csv('sales_data.csv', index=False)
      print('CSV generated: sales_data.csv')
      ```
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload via Dashboard" icon="upload">
    Click "Upload Dataset" on dashboard, drag-and-drop your CSV, or select file. Name it (e.g., "Q1 Sales") and click Upload.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validate Upload" icon="check-circle">
    Review the preview table. Alchemetryx auto-detects columns and data types. Fix issues if prompted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Run Your First Data Exploration

With data uploaded, explore insights in seconds.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Dataset" icon="database">
    From Datasets list, click your file (e.g., "Q1 Sales").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Insights" icon="zap">
    Click "Analyze Now". Alchemetryx runs auto-insights: top products, revenue trends, regional breakdowns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Views" icon="bar-chart-3">
    Filter by region (UK/India), add charts (bar/pie), or export as PDF.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="success">
  Congratulations! You've completed your first analysis. Insights appear in under 30 seconds.
</Callout>
