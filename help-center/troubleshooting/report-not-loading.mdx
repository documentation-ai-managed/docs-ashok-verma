---
title: Report Not Loading
description: Fix Alchemetryx report loading issues.
---

## Symptoms
- Dashboard stays blank
- No insights shown after submitting data

## Steps to Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh the page">
    ````tip
    Use `Ctrl + F5` to perform a hard reload.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your data format">
    ````info
    Ensure your file is CSV or Excel, under 10MB.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try another browser">
    ````tip
    Chrome or Edge work best with Alchemetryx.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
